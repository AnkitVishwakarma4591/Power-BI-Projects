# **Power BI Projects**  

## **1. BlinkIT Sales Dashboard**  
### **Overview**  
This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of **BlinkIT's** sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet distribution. It helps businesses track sales trends, product demand, and outlet efficiency.  

### **Key Insights**  
🔹 **Total Sales:** $1.20M revenue generated.  
🔹 **Customer Trends:** 8,523 total items sold with an average rating of 3.9.  
🔹 **Outlet Performance:** Breakdown by size and location (Tier 1, 2, 3).  
🔹 **Product Trends:** High sales in **Fruits & Vegetables, Snack Foods, and Household items**.  
🔹 **Outlet Establishment:** Trend analysis from 2012 to 2022.  

### **Features**  
✅ **Interactive filters** for outlet location, size, and item type.  
✅ **Sales breakdown** based on fat content, outlet type, and item categories.  
✅ **Time-series analysis** to track growth over the years.  
✅ **Region-wise insights** with outlet size and location comparison.
